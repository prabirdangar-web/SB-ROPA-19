5. Leave Account (Register)

Uses the Name, Designation and Date of Joining entered in Section 1 above. Set a retirement / generate-up-to date below (defaults to the DOR already calculated in Section 1).

5.1 Auto-Generate Period of Duty Rows

Generates annual (365/366-day) spans anchored to the 1st DOJ anniversary, right up to the date below. Earned Leave (avg pay) and HPL (half avg pay) are each credited at 15 days per full 365-day span, prorated for part-spans. EL is capped at 365 days lifetime; HPL credit caps at 60 days (earning pauses while at cap, and resumes — up to the cap — after leave is taken).

Enter each new-school joining date. The old-school row will automatically end the day before it; the underlying annual anniversary cycle keeps counting from the original 1st DOJ regardless.

5.2 Period of Duty Rows

Add or edit each duty-period row manually if needed. "Leave earned on" adds to the running credit at the start of that period; "Leave at Credit on" for Row 1 can be entered manually (opening balance), every later row carries forward automatically.

5.3 Add Leave Entry (ML / HPL / LWP) — feeds Leave A/c and all ROPA tables

Posted into the Leave Account (split across period rows if it crosses a year-end) and noted in the "Event / Reason" column of every ROPA-1998/2009/2019 row whose Effective Date–Date of Termination window overlaps these dates. For option 1 ("LWP — WITH effect on 10/18/20Y increment"), every ROPA-1998/2009/2019 increment and 10/18/20-Year milestone date falling on or after this entry's start date is automatically shifted to account for it — multiple LWP spells across the whole career are all added up, there's no need to enter anything separately in Sections 2–4.
